Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Vardhaman Agencies collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our packaging solutions and services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you interact with Vardhaman Agencies, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, business name, phone number, email address, and business address
  • Business Information: Company details, GST number, industry type, and purchasing requirements
  • Account Information: Login credentials for customer portal, order history, and preferences
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely by our payment partners)
  • Communication Records: Email correspondence, phone call logs, and customer service interactions

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, click patterns, and referral sources
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information stored through cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Vardhaman Agencies uses your information for the following purposes:

Business Operations

  • Order Processing: Processing and fulfilling your bubble wrap and packaging orders
  • Customer Service: Responding to inquiries, providing support, and resolving issues
  • Account Management: Managing your customer account and preferences
  • Payment Processing: Processing payments and maintaining financial records

Communication

  • Order Updates: Sending order confirmations, shipping notifications, and delivery updates
  • Product Information: Sharing new product offerings and packaging solutions
  • Business Updates: Important company announcements and policy changes
  • Marketing Communications: Promotional offers and industry insights (with your consent)

Business Improvement

  • Website Enhancement: Improving our website functionality and user experience
  • Product Development: Developing new packaging solutions based on customer needs
  • Analytics: Understanding customer behavior and preferences
  • Quality Assurance: Monitoring and improving our service quality

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Shipping Partners: Delivery companies for order fulfillment
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing services
  • IT Services: Cloud hosting, security, and technical support providers
  • Business Partners: Trusted suppliers and logistics partners

Legal Requirements

  • Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Enforcement: To enforce our terms of service or policies

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

Data Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your personal information.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and assessments to maintain the highest security standards.

Secure Infrastructure

Our systems are hosted on secure, monitored infrastructure with 24/7 protection.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, security, and to remember your preferences during your visit.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve performance and user experience.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access & Review

Request access to your personal information and review how it's being used.

Correction & Update

Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and business requirements.

Data Portability

Request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.

Consent Withdrawal

Withdraw consent for marketing communications and certain data processing activities.

Restriction

Request restrictions on how your personal information is processed.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

Notification of Changes

  • Website Notice: We will post the updated policy on our website with the new effective date
  • Email Notification: For significant changes, we will notify you via email
  • Continued Use: Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance

Review Recommendations

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
